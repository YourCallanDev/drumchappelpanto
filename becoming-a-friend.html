Becoming a Friend

The Drumchapel Pantomime Club Friends Membership Scheme offers priority booking, exclusive discounts and supports the club's future. Members get advanced booking, no in-house booking fees, opening night discounts, newsletters, and invites to Friends-only events.

Your Membership Includes:

  • Advanced booking ahead of the general public
  • No booking fees on in-house productions
  • Opening night discounted tickets on selected shows
  • Regular club newsletters
  • Invitations to exclusive events

Friends can also get involved in every aspect of the theatre — including performing onstage and helping behind the scenes. Life membership is available for a one-off fee. Memberships start at £30 per year.

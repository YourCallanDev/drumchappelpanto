Becoming a Friend

The Drumchapel Pantomime Group Friends Membership Scheme offers you priority booking, exclusive discounts, and the knowledge that being a Friend plays a vital role in the future development of the club.

Your Membership Includes:

  • Advanced booking ahead of the general public
  • No booking fees on in-house productions
  • Opening night discounted tickets on selected shows
  • Regular club newsletters with updates onstage and behind the scenes
  • Invitations to exclusive ‘Friends Only’ social events

Friends can also get involved in every aspect of the theatre — including performing onstage and helping behind the scenes. We also offer life membership for a one-off fee!

Drumchapel Pantomime Group welcomes everyone to become a Friend of the theatre.

If you are interested in becoming a Friend, please get in touch and we can arrange your membership. Annual memberships start at £30 per year.

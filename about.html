About Us

In 2022, Maggie Harkness-Smith launched Pantozonians and presented Cinderella at the Goodyear Social Club in Drumchapel. The group has since run fundraisers and in 2023 renamed to Drumchapel Amateur Pantomime Club, mounting The Wizard of Oz. Rehearsals run April–December each year and all are welcome.

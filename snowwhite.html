Snow White – 2025 Production

Show Information

Get ready for a magical, laugh-out-loud panto packed with singing, dancing, and dazzling costumes! Join us as Drumchapel Amateur Panto Club brings a hilarious twist to the classic fairytale.

Dates & Times

Saturday 22nd & Sunday 23rd November 2025

1:15PM Doors Open – 2:00PM Show Starts

Facilities

Tuck Shop at interval by Kirstie Docherty
Bar available before & after the show

Location

Goodyear Social Club, Glasgow, G15 6SA

Open in Maps

Accessibility & Parking

Wheelchair accessible. Parking is limited — please arrive early where possible.

Runtime

  • Act 1 — 40 minutes
  • Interval — 20 minutes
  • Act 2 — 30 minutes

Credits

  • Written & Directed by: Maggie Harkness-Smith
  • Technically Directed by: Callan Chesser
  • Makeup Artists: Nidine Boyle

FAQ

Are tickets still available?

Tickets are fully sold out, but we can add you to a waiting list at the door.

When is the bar open?

The bar will be open before & after the show, and during our 20 minute interval!

Can we talk during the preformance?

Of course, however we ask that you keep the talking to a minimum otherwise we may need to ask you to leave!

Is the show suitable for young children?

Yes it is. Infact, it was written for kids so they can have a great time at their local panto!

Are photos allowed?

Photos are allowed, and the cast might even stay after to get some photos with you! Please try and avoid flash during the preformance.

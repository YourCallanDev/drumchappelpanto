Meet the Team

Maggie Harkness-Smith

Group Founder & Artistic Director

Maggie Harkness-Smith is a talented scriptwriter and director, with a passion for creating unforgettable pantomimes and shows. As the founder of the club, she has brought joy and laughter to audiences of all ages. With her expertise and creativity. Maggie is sure to make every production a success.

Andrew McVie

Assistant Director

Andrew McVie is an experienced Assistant Director for shows, with a background in theatre production. He previously ran the Stardust Theatre Company and is now lending his expertise to the Drumchapel Pantomime Group. With his passion for theatre and dedication to his craft, Andrew is sure to bring a wealth of knowledge and creativity to any production he works on.

Jackie Doyle

Backstage Manager

Jackie Doyle is our backstage manager extraordinaire! She's here to make sure that all cast members are in the right place at the right time. With her help, our performances will run like clockwork.

Callan Chesser

Technical Director

Callan Chesser is our technical director. He makes sure all of the tech for the shows work, and has it all programmed in and ready for the show. During the show he can be found running it all and making all of our beautiful cast members heard. With a true passion for the theatre and the technical elements, Callan is sure to bring a lot of knowledge and creativy to the productions!
